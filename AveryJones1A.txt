Program Description:
This program Sells a set number of tickets (20) with a purchas limit of 4.
After all tickets are purchased the program will show the total number of buyers.

---

logical steps:
1. prompt user to enter desired amount of tickets
2. check if desired amount is between 1 and 4 and if the amount desired is more than avalable
3. subtract desired ticket amount from total amount of tickets and add 1 to total amount of buyers
4. repeat steps 1-3 until all tickets are sold
5. print total number of buyers

---

variables:
1. total_tickets: The total amount of tickets avalable to buy
2. total_buyers: the total amount of people to buy tickets
3. desired_tickets: the amount of tickets a buyer wants

---

Functions:
1. Function Name: pre_sell_tickets
   Description: Sells a set amount of tickets and counts total amount of buyers
   Parameters: none
   returns: total count of buyers
